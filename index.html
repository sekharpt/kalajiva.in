Every year, millions of people in India lose income because of extreme weather — like heavy rain, heatwaves, or floods — with no safety net. Most insurance companies focus on high-value customers. That leaves informal workers, small farmers, gig workers, and micro-entrepreneurs without any meaningful protection.

Kalajiva is building a solution to fix that.

We are a unified insurtech platform that helps insurers offer climate-linked insurance to underserved communities. These insurance products are simple, automated, and fast — using real-time weather data to trigger instant payouts when an event occurs. No paperwork. No delays.

Kalajiva is currently incubated at the Indian Institute of Information Technology – Atal Incubation Centre, Kottayam and actively working with insurers to build inclusive, climate-resilient insurance pilots.

Our Solution

Our solution is a unified insurtech platform purpose-built to enable simple, event-triggered insurance to underserved communities. We help insurers design and distribute parametric products that offer fast, paperless payouts during climate shocks like heavy rainfall, heatwaves, or floods. By integrating real-time data and partnering with grassroots networks — Kalajiva makes climate-linked protection accessible, affordable, and scalable.
