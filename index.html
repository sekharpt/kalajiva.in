Across India, millions face daily income loss from unpredictable climate events — without any financial protection. Mostly insurers focus on high value customers leading to inadequate technology access for micro markets. Kalajiva is changing that.

Kalajiva is a unified insurtech platform that brings smart, trigger-based insurance solutions to the underserved and uninsured affected by Natural Catastrophes. We partner with leading insurers and distribution networks to deliver parametric insurance — simple, fast, and transparent coverage triggered by real-world data.

We are incubated at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam - Atul Incubation Center.

Our Solution

Our solution is a platform that connects insurers, intermediaries, and micro-consumers, enabling seamless distribution, policy management, and digital documentation for parametric insurance solutions for the underinsured/uninsured. Kalajiva enables automated, event-triggered insurance that pays users when extreme weather conditions disrupt their work — no claims, no documents, no waiting.
